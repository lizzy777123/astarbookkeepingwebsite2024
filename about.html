Who We Are

Luxury Gifting & Event Décor Agency


Simply put, We create great event memories!

Awera Visuals is a international dual event decor and luxury gifting agency; specialising in professional event planning, customized balloon decorations and luxury money bouquets. Dallas Fort Worth (DFW), Texas metroplex.

We are a Dallas Fort Worth (DFW), Texas based, minority, female-owned business, committed to providing innovative and professional visual solutions for individuals, businesses, and government agencies.

The name "Awera", meaning "beautiful" in Yoruba, reflects our passion for crafting affordable yet stunning event decorations and luxury money bouquets that leave you saying “wow.” Book your next event with Awera Visuals and leave your guests overwhelmed with joy and unforgettable memories.

Meet our Founder

Awera Visuals was initially founded by Elizabeth, also known as Lizzy POPM MSc FCCA BA in London, UK before a brand refresh and expansion to Dallas, TX. Lizzy is a seasoned professional with a strong background in data analytics, finance, and creative design.

She holds the following credentials:

  • Certified Product Owner
  • Master’s in Data Science and Analytics (1st Class Honours)
  • Master’s in Accounting and Finance (2:1)
  • Bachelors in Accounting and Finance (1st Class Honours)
  • ACCA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants) Chartered Accountant UK (Fellow Member)

Lizzy’s expertise in aviation, finance, analytics and branding ensures that every project is delivered with precision, cost-effectiveness, and exceptional quality.
